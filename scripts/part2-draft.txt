THE TAG TEAM — PART 2

## Setup
Hours later. Riley's been locked the WHOLE time. Kneeling. Waiting. Desperate.

## The Return

The door opens.

"You're still here?" Elara laughs, stepping inside with Ren behind her. "God, look at him. hasn't moved."

Ren circles him slowly, circling him like prey. "Such a good boy. Kept his position all day."

Riley whimpers.

"We have a *special* treat for you today," Elara murmurs, crouching down to his level. "Ren and I have been talking... and we think you've earned a promotion."

She looks up at Ren. Ren grins.

"TWICE the attention. One of us each side." Ren drops to her knees behind him, hands sliding up his sides. "Or maybe we take turns. I use him first, Elara watches and degrades. Then Elara finishes while I coach."

"How do you want it, baby?" Elara taps his cage lightly. "Tell us."

Riley can barely speak.

"Oh he likes that," Ren laughs. "He's shaking."

## The Session

Ren moves first — turns him, presses him down. "You're *our* toy today. Say it."

"I'm— I'm your toy," Riley gasps.

Elara watches from the corner, arms crossed, smirking. "Look at him. So small. So *tiny*. And Ren, look at this—" She holds up her strap, comparing it to his caged form. "We *both* outpace him in every way."

Ren laughs breathlessly, thrusting. "God, *yes*. He's so pathetic. Built for servicing *us*."

They switch. Elara takes over while Ren watches, coaching. "Faster. Make him cry."

When they're done — both finished, both satisfied — they stand over him.

"We're not done though," Ren says softly. "We're gonna leave you like this."

"No— *please*—"

Elara tilts her head. "Please *what*, pet?"

"Please... please don't leave..."

"**Please don't leave.**" Ren mocks him. "Aww. But we've used you up. You're empty. Go kneel in the corner and think about what you've done."

They leave.

The door clicks shut.

He stays locked overnight.
